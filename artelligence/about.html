About us

Empowering People By Keeping Them Well

ETain is a vibrant network that convenes government, academia, businesses, and civil society to create a collaborative AI ecosystem focused on education, ethics, economy, and innovation-benefiting industry and individuals in KZN.

  • Promote research, adoption, and ethical use of AI technologies.
  • Harness AI for economic growth, creating business and career opportunities while addressing local socio-economic issues.
  • Provide accessible education and training to individuals, organizations, and government.
  • Collaborate across sectors to co-create solutions prioritizing social needs over profit.
  • Ensure AI contributes positively to sectors including government, maritime, healthcare, agriculture, manufacturing, logistics, education, tourism, wholesale, and retail.
